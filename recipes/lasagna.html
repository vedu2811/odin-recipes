Lasagna

Descripton:

Lasagna, a beloved Italian classic, features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese. Begin by preparing the meat sauce: brown ground beef with onions, garlic, tomato paste, canned tomatoes, and Italian herbs. Simmer to enhance flavors. Meanwhile, make the béchamel sauce by melting butter, adding flour, then milk until thickened. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

To assemble, layer the meat sauce, lasagna noodles, béchamel, and cheeses in a baking dish, repeating until filled, ending with béchamel and cheese. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly. Rest before serving with salad and garlic bread.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Make the Bechamel Sauce
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
  6. Rest and Serve
