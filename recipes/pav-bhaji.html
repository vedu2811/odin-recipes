Pav-Bhaji

Descripton:

Pav bhaji, a Mumbai street food favorite, brings together a medley of vegetables cooked to perfection with a blend of aromatic spices. Sautéed in butter and seasoned with onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies, the vegetables form a flavorful base. A symphony of pav bhaji masala, turmeric, chili powder, and cumin infuses the dish with a spicy and fragrant allure. Coarsely mashed, the vegetables create a thick and hearty bhaji, ready to be savored alongside buttered pav bread rolls.

Fresh off the griddle, the pav bread rolls are toasted to a golden crispness, their buttery aroma enticing with every bite. When served alongside the steaming hot bhaji, the contrast of textures and flavors is pure bliss. Topped with chopped onions, fragrant cilantro, and a squeeze of lime, this iconic street food dish promises a taste of Mumbai's vibrant culinary scene, delivering satisfaction in every mouthful.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cauliflower
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Salt
  • Bell peppers
  • Butter
  • Green Chillies
  • Pav bhaji masala
  • Turmeric
  • Chilli powder
  • Lime
  • Pav bread rolls

Steps:

  1. Dice potatoes, chop cauliflower, carrots, and bell peppers. Sauté onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies in butter until soft.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until tender. Sprinkle with pav bhaji masala, turmeric, chili powder, and cumin.
  3. Mash vegetables coarsely and simmer to blend flavors.
  4. Slice pav bread rolls, spread with butter, and toast until golden.
  5. Serve hot bhaji with toasted pav.
  6. Garnish with onions, cilantro, and lime.
